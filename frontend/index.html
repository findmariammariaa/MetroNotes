About MetroNotes

MetroNotes is a student-built platform created to make academic life easier for students at Metropolitan University. We believe in open collaboration and easy access to educational resources.

Whether you're preparing for exams or organizing class notes, MetroNotes supports you with intuitive design, department-based organization, and a smooth upload system. We aim to make studying collaborative, accessible, and efficient.
